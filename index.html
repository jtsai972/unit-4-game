Instructions

Below are four crystals of differing values. Click them to trade them in for points.

In order to win, you must match the total points to the goal number.

You will lose if your total score goes above the goal number.

Each crystal's value is unknown until you trade it in.

Everytime the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.
